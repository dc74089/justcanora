PyCharm -- Setup Instructions

This page will walk you through how to set up PyCharm.

Please read each step carefully.

Step 1: Download PyCharm

  1. Open Manager
  2. Find "PyCharm" (make sure it says Mosyle Catalog)
  3. Click "Install" (if you see "Reinstall", it's already installed!)

Step 2: Enter License Info

  1. Open PyCharm
  2. It should ask you for a license. If it does not, click the gear in the bottom left, then click "Manage Licenses"
  3. Choose "License Server"
  4. Enter https://lhps.fls.jetbrains.com/ in the "Server Address" box.
  5. Click "Activate"

Step 2: Log In

  1. In the window that opens, click the blue/green icon. You don't need to enter your username or password.
